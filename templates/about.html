About Job Fit Pro

This app helps you analyze your resume and calculate its ATS (Applicant Tracking System) score.

We extract your skills, qualifications, and experience to match your resume against job descriptions using smart algorithms.

Once uploaded, your resume is parsed, and job recommendations are shown based on your unique skill set.

Our goal is to help you improve your chances of getting noticed by employers and landing interviews faster.

Whether you're a fresher or an experienced professional, Resume Parser gives you insights and actionable feedback.
